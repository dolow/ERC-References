ERC 20 Sample

This sample provides ERC20 based token faucet.

Instruction

  • Get some testnet ETH from faucet.
  • Enter amount of "Nice Token (NT)" you want.
  • Press "Free Mint" button,
  • Entered NT is minted and transfered to your wallet.
  • Import ERC20 token address to show NT as your asset in the wallet.

* Instruction above is based on Metamask UI.
